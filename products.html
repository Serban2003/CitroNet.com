Oranges

      The orange is the fruit of various citrus species. The orange is a hybrid between pomelo and mandarin.
      The orange is known for having a sweet-tart taste.

Mandarins

      The mandarin is a small citrus tree fruit.
      Mandarins are smaller and oblate, unlike the spherical common oranges. The taste is considered less sour, as well as sweeter and stronger than that of an orange.

Clementines

      A clementine is a tangor, a citrus fruit hybrid between a mandarin and an orange.
      They are typically juicy and sweet, with less acid than oranges.

Tangerines

      The tangerine is a species of mandarin.
      Tangerines are smaller and less rounded than common oranges. They’re sweeter than navel oranges but still a bit tart.
